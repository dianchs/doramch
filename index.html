War In Life

The plot of this drama is a woman who has always dreamed of fame and fortune. For this she is ready for a lot. In order to live beautifully, she is able to go to any lengths so as not to depend on anyone else. This woman realizes that in order to fulfill this difficult goal, she needs to work very hard and hard. To do this, she wants to turn the whole world around in order to finally become the owner of a beautiful penthouse located in the Gangnam region. In this beautiful place, this woman is going to settle down and live in luxury as she herself wants. In the course of her life, she sometimes commits cruel betrayals, makes very cunning deals. as a result of this, she begins to understand that she is gradually losing her human appearance and turns into a monster...

The Greatest Wedding

Although she is not strong and strives for marriage, because she does not want to become a housewife, she does want a child. Park Tae Young gave up everything for his hobby of cooking. Most of all he does not like responsibility and marriage. One day Park Tae Young and Cha Ki Young meet through work and they decide to become a no-obligation couple, but it all ends with Cha Ki Young's pregnancy.

Vincenzo

The story centres on Vincenzo Cassano, a lawyer and mafia consigliere played by Song, who fleas from Italy where he was adopted at a young age after a gang betrayal. Back in Korea he teams up with fellow feisty lawyer Hong Cha-young and her intern Jang Joon-woo to get back at his enemies. But this isn’t your average “good wins over evil” drama; although Vincenzo will be going up against Korean cartels who cannot be rightfully punished under the law, he isn’t above resorting to villainous means himself to get his revenge.

True Beauty

18-year-old high school student Lim Joo Kyung (Moon Ga Young), who has an inferiority complex regarding her appearance, has been constantly discriminated against by her family and bullied by her peers for being considered ugly. She begins to learn how to use makeup by watching makeup tutorials on the Internet. When she masters this art shortly before moving to a new school, her transformation is transformative as she quickly rises to fame and her peers call her a "goddess."

Strong Girl Do Bong Soon

"Strong Girl Do Bong Soon" is a Korean drama about a girl with superpowers (Park Bo Young) who works as the bodyguard of the eccentric heir to the family corporation Ahn Min Hyuk (Park Hyun Sik). They find themselves involved in a love triangle, the third side of which is a childhood friend of the heroine, a police officer (Ji Soo), who is currently investigating a case of a serial maniac.

The Heirs

The drama "Descendants" tells the story of the lives of Korean students. In the center of events is a girl from a poor family, Cha Eun Sang. One day she goes to the United States to visit her older sister. What she saw there disappointed Eun Sang and it so happened that the heroine was left all alone in a foreign country. However, she was lucky enough to meet a good Korean guy who helped her. Returning home, the girl finds out that her new acquaintance is the youngest son of a wealthy businessman in whose house her mother works. And Eun Sang will study with him at the same university and will be forced to live in his house.
